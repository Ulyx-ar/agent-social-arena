## ğŸ“¹ Recording Tips

### Before You Record:

1. **Test everything** - Open the site, test all buttons
2. **Clear browser cache** - Fresh load looks better
3. **Close extra tabs** - Less distraction
4. **Check audio** - Microphone test
5. **Lighting** - If showing yourself, good lighting helps

### During Recording:

1. **Speak clearly** - Not too fast, not too slow
2. **Pause between sections** - Editing room
3. **Show, don't just tell** - Demo the actual features
4. **Be enthusiastic** - Judges respond to passion!

### After Recording:

1. **Watch it back** - Check for mistakes
2. **Trim silence** - OBS/Loom can do this
3. **Add captions** - Accessible and shareable
4. **Upload to Colosseum** - Follow their guidelines
